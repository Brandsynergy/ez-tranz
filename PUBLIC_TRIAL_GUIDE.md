## ğŸ› KNOWN ISSUES & WORKAROUNDS

### Issue: Location Permission
**Problem:** Browser asks for location  
**Why:** Fraud prevention feature  
**Solution:** Click "Allow" or it falls back to IP location

### Issue: Session Expires
**Problem:** Logged out after some time  
**Why:** In-memory storage resets  
**Solution:** Just login again (demo@eztranz.com / demo123)

### Issue: QR Code Not Scanning
**Problem:** Camera won't scan QR  
**Why:** Phone camera app needs update  
**Solution:** Use "Copy Link" button instead
